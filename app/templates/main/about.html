Beauty is broken in Nigeria

Most beauty service bookings happen through WhatsApp DMs, Instagram messages, and phone calls. There's no standardized platform, no verified quality, and no protection for clients or providers.

Low-income users are priced out of home services, while talented independent professionals struggle to build steady clientele. We built BookGlam to fix all of that.
